<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Golden Energy Pipe is the high-throughput conduit, carrying up to 1,280 E/t by default. Use it for the trunk lines that feed your most demanding machines.

E is fed into the network by a Wooden Energy Pipe and delivered to any adjacent machine that consumes E.
<link to="buildcraft:pipe/wood_rf"/>

Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can stop pipes connecting.
