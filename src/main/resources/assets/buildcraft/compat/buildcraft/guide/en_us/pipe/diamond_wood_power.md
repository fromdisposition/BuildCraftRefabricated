<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Wooden Diamond Kinesis Pipe works just like the Wooden Kinesis Pipe - it is the input that draws power out of an engine or other source and hands it to the network - but carries far more, up to 256 MJ/t by default.
<link to="buildcraft:pipe/wood_power"/>

Unlike the Wooden Diamond Transport Pipe there is nothing to filter, since power has no item types; the diamond simply raises the transfer rate. It will not connect to another wooden power pipe.
