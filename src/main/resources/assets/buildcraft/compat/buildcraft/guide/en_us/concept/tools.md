<lore>
Every engineer needs a toolkit — for turning machines, painting pipes, and reading what has gone wrong.
</lore>
<no_lore>
BuildCraft's utility items help you configure, paint and debug your machines.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="The Wrench"/>
The <link inline="buildcraft:item/wrench"/> rotates machines and pipes and sets their working face — the output side of an iron pipe, or the direction an engine points. Right-click a block to cycle it.
<chapter name="Painting & Lists"/>
The <link inline="buildcraft:item/paintbrush"/> colours pipes and items once loaded with dye, so daizuli pipes and lenses can sort by colour. The <link inline="buildcraft:item/list"/> records a set of items as a reusable filter you can copy into machines and gates.
<chapter name="Marking & Debugging"/>
The <link inline="buildcraft:item/marker_connector"/> links markers into regions and paths — see <link to="buildcraft:concept/markers"/>. The <link inline="buildcraft:item/debugger"/> prints a block's internal state, which is invaluable when a machine misbehaves.
