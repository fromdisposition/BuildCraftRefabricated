<lore>
A pipe has six faces, and each can wear a small device — a gate, a lens, a cover, a sensor — without taking up a whole block of space.
</lore>
<no_lore>
Pluggables attach to the faces of a pipe: facades, plugs, lenses, gates, pulsars and sensors.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Covers & Blockers"/>
A <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_facade"/> wraps a pipe face to look like any block, so you can hide pipework inside walls. A <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_blocker"/> seals a face so nothing can enter or leave there — useful with a wooden pipe to force items in one direction.
<chapter name="Filters & Logic"/>
A <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_lens"/> tints items passing a face, or filters by colour; with daizuli pipes this sorts painted goods. The <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_gate"/> is how logic gates attach to pipes — the full system is in <link to="buildcraft:concept/gates_and_wires"/>.
<chapter name="Power & Sensing"/>
A <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_pulsar"/> turns a steady redstone or power signal into pulses — ideal for running a redstone engine continuously. A <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_light_sensor"/> reads the surrounding light level as a gate trigger, and a <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_power_adaptor"/> exposes a pipe's power to an adjacent machine.
