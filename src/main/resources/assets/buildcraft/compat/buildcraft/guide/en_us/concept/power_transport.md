<lore>
Kinesis pipes are wires for mechanical power — they don't store MJ, they pass it straight through to whatever needs it.
</lore>
<no_lore>
Kinesis pipes carry MJ from engines to machines; a separate set of pipes carries Forge Energy (FE/RF).
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Kinesis Pipes"/>
Start a power line with a <link inline="buildcraft:pipe/wood_power"/> on an engine's piston face, then extend it with <link inline="buildcraft:pipe/cobble_power"/>, stone and the rest. Higher tiers carry more MJ/t before losing energy as heat: the <link inline="buildcraft:pipe/gold_power"/> moves a great deal, and the <link inline="buildcraft:pipe/diamond_power"/> lets you direct power to chosen sides.
<chapter name="Limiting Power"/>
Some machines overload if over-fed. The iron and diamond kinesis pipes can cap throughput with a gate action — see <link to="buildcraft:concept/set_power_limit"/> — so a strong engine can safely supply a delicate machine.
<chapter name="Forge Energy"/>
To carry FE/RF instead of MJ, use the energy pipes (<link inline="buildcraft:pipe/wood_rf"/> and up). Convert between the two systems with the <link inline="buildcraft:block/engine_rf"/> and the <link inline="buildcraft:block/mj_dynamo"/>. The engines that produce MJ in the first place are covered in <link to="buildcraft:concept/power_mj"/>.
