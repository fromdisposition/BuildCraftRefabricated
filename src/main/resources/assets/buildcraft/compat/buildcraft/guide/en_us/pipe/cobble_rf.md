<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Cobblestone Energy Pipe is the cheapest conduit, carrying up to 40 E/t by default - the lowest rate of any Energy Pipe - so it is best kept to low-throughput runs.

E is fed into the network by a Wooden Energy Pipe and delivered to any adjacent machine that consumes E.
<link to="buildcraft:pipe/wood_rf"/>

Cobblestone Energy Pipes will not connect to Stone or Quartz Energy Pipes.
Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can also stop pipes connecting.
