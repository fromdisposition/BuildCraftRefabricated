<lore>
Lasers and silicon tables are BuildCraft's tech tier — where you etch chipsets and program the gates that run everything else.
</lore>
<no_lore>
The silicon machines use focused laser power to craft chipsets, gates and other advanced components.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Laser & Assembly"/>
A <link inline="buildcraft:block/laser"/> fires energy into a nearby <link inline="buildcraft:block/assembly_table"/>, which slowly assembles its selected recipe from that laser power. Several lasers can feed one table to speed it up. This is how you produce <link inline="buildcraft:item/chipset_redstone"/> and the other chipsets.
<chapter name="The Tables"/>
The <link inline="buildcraft:block/integration_table"/> combines items — adding upgrades or programming gates. The <link inline="buildcraft:block/advanced_crafting_table"/> is a powered auto-crafter. The <link inline="buildcraft:block/programming_table"/> writes gate logic and robot boards, while the <link inline="buildcraft:block/charging_table"/> recharges redstone-powered items.
<chapter name="Programming Gates"/>
Use these tables to etch chipsets and assemble the <link inline="buildcraft:item/plug_gate"/> tiers, then clip them onto pipes to build automation — see <link to="buildcraft:concept/gates_and_wires"/>. The boards you program here also drive the drones in <link to="buildcraft:concept/robots_and_zones"/>.
