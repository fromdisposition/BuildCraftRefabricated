<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Wooden Diamond Energy Pipe works just like the Wooden Energy Pipe - it is the input that draws E out of a source and hands it to the network - but carries far more, up to 2,560 E/t by default.
<link to="buildcraft:pipe/wood_rf"/>

E has no item types to filter, so the diamond simply raises the transfer rate over the Wooden Energy Pipe. It will not connect to another wooden Energy Pipe.

<chapter name="Powering"/>
An MJ Dynamo or any other E source can feed it.
<link to="buildcraft:block/mj_dynamo"/>
