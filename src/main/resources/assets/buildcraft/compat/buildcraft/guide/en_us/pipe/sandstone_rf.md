<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Sandstone Energy Pipe carries up to 160 E/t by default. Its special trait is that it connects only to other pipes - never to a machine, engine, or other block beside it.

This lets an energy line run right past a block you do not want to feed, where any other Energy Pipe would deliver power into it.

E is fed into the network by a Wooden Energy Pipe.
<link to="buildcraft:pipe/wood_rf"/>
Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can also stop pipes connecting.
